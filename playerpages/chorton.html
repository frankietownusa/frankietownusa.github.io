Cade Horton

About

Cade Horton was a multi-sport athlete at the University of Oklahoma, where he was both a football player in addition to a pitcher and third baseman in baseball. Before his freshman season, he tore his UCL and underwent Tommy John surgery. While he rehabbed, he pitched in relief and played third base, but, when he was fully healthy, was moved to the starting rotation.

In 2022, he faced 232 batters in 53.2 innings, and gave up 8 home runs and 29 RBI while striking out 64. Batters averaged .267 against him. He finished the season with a 4.86 ERA, 1.304 WHIP, and 4.27 SO/W.
